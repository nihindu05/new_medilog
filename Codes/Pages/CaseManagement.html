ⓘ Registration Guidelines

  • Fields marked with * are mandatory.
  • Verify patient/victim identity matches the MLEF or Inquest Order before saving.
  • Clinical cases require a valid MLEF number and issuing police station.
  • Autopsy cases require an Inquest Order or Court Order before registration.
  • Flag cases involving minors or sexual assault as Restricted immediately.
  • Ensure chain of custody is marked when collecting forensic samples or evidence.
  • Police and court references must be linked before any report submission.
  • All changes are recorded in audit logs for legal accountability.
