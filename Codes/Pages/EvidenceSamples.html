Evidence Notes

ⓘ Evidence Handling Rules

  • Every sample should be linked to a valid case and examination.
  • Record seal number, storage condition and collection time before transfer.
  • Use chain of custody for every handover between doctor, lab, police or court.
  • Lab results should be entered later through the Lab / Toxicology page.
  • Do not delete evidence records; archive them for legal traceability.
