ⓘ Patient / Victim Registration Guidelines

  • Fields marked with * are mandatory.
  • Separate living victim details from deceased person/body receiving details.
  • Minor, sexual assault, unidentified, and court-priority records should use restricted access flags.
  • Always confirm NIC/passport, hospital no, BHT no, and next of kin details where available.
  • Link all related clinical or autopsy cases so the detail view shows the complete medico-legal history.
